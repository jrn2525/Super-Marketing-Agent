3. **Trainer** — A trainer steps back and observes the process. A trainer not only teaches but also builds a structure that allows others to learn. This is where the mastery of communication, environment creation, and systematized learning takes place.

4. **Soul** — This is where real transformation happens. You are not just processing knowledge intellectually; you are experiencing it at a soul level. When something reaches the soul, it becomes part of your identity. The goal is not to know but to **become.**

People don't struggle because they lack knowledge; they struggle because they haven't shifted their identity. Why are most people overweight when losing weight is simple? This same principle applies to money, relationships, and every area of life. If you've never been wealthy, your identity is rooted in struggle. You must rewire yourself to **become** the person who commands wealth, power, and success.

Understanding is not enough; execution matters. The Learning Cycle ensures true mastery:

Dominion is about power—your power over your body, mind, relationships, and business. You are either leaking power or consolidating it. Your results—physical, financial, and relational—reflect your dominion. If you are not where you want to be, you are **losing power somewhere.**

This is not a seminar, a motivational talk, or just another training. This is a **call to arms.** Over the next chapters, we will break down every step you need to take to claim your dominion and become unstoppable.

## Chapter 3: THE FOUR Ps

At the core of the Warrior Method is **The Code**—a framework that governs how we live, lead, and influence. Without The Code, all strategies, skills, and tactics fall apart. The Code is what unlocks **truth, power, and transformation**.

The concept of The Code isn't just a vague philosophy—it's a **practical system** that creates **immediate, measurable impact** in your business, relationships, and personal life.

The foundational principle of The Code is simple:

**The facts will set you free.**

When you live in **truth**, you gain **power**. When you lie—to yourself or others—you lose power. The world is filled with people **pretending** to be happy, successful, or fulfilled. They are **not free**. They are prisoners of their own deception. The goal of The Warrior Method is to **destroy illusion and unlock reality**, because *where truth is, God is*—and where God is, transformation is possible.

### The Four Ps Of The Code

The Code is built on **four core principles**, which create **clarity, power, and action** in any situation:

1. **Real Facts — (*What is the truth?*)**
   - The first step to any breakthrough is **acknowledging reality**.
   - This means stripping away **emotions, stories, and justifications** and looking at raw, objective facts.
   - **Your truth changes, but facts do not.**
   - **Example:** A husband rates his marriage an **8 out of 10**, while his wife rates it a **2 out of 10**. His perception is irrelevant; her reality is her reality. *Both are true.*
   - Until people can **admit their real facts**, they cannot change their lives.

2. **Raw Feelings — (*How do I feel about the facts?*)**
   - Once the facts are laid out, the next step is to access the **emotional impact** of those facts.
   - **Most people suppress their feelings** or disguise them with logic and avoidance.
   - **Example:** A man avoids acknowledging that he feels **weak, ashamed, and insecure** about his business failing. Instead, he covers it with bravado or blame.
   - **Your feelings don't have to be logical, but they must be acknowledged.**
   - Suppressing emotions **creates internal war**, but **owning them creates power**.

3. **Relevant Focus — (*What do I want next?*)**
   - Most people are **clear on what they don't want** but **have no idea what they do want**.
   - **Transformation requires a clear target.**
   - **Example:** A man says he "doesn't want to be broke," but what does he actually want? Does he want to make $250,000 a year? $1 million? $10 million?
   - Without clear **focus**, people remain trapped in frustration.
   - **Powerful people know exactly what they want.**

4. **Result-Driven Fruit — (*What measurable result will prove I have changed?*)**
   - Desire without action is **delusion**.
   - Every transformation must have a **tangible, measurable result**.
   - **Example:** A man says he wants a stronger marriage. How will he know he has succeeded? More intimacy? More date nights? Less fighting?
   - **Success must be defined in numbers, actions, and outcomes.**
   - Without **measurable fruit**, growth is an illusion.

### The Power Of Living By The Code

When you align with The Code, everything changes:

- **You stop lying to yourself and others.**
- **You see people's lies instantly.**
- **You become unshakable in business and relationships.**
- **You lead with power instead of weakness.**

Living by The Code means:

1. **Clarifying the Real Facts** — No stories, no justifications. Only truth.
2. **Owning the Raw Feelings** — No suppression. Acknowledge what is real.
3. **Declaring the Relevant Focus** — Get crystal clear on what you want next.
4. **Producing Result-Driven Fruit** — Prove change through measurable action.

This framework is **the backbone** of every skill inside The Warrior Method. Whether you are closing a client, leading a team, or fixing your marriage—**The Code is the key to power**.

### The Big Lie: What Are You Pretending Isn't True?

Most people are **trapped in lies they tell themselves.**

**Mastering The Code = Mastering Influence**

## Chapter 4: THE SACRED

Once you step into truth, a new power opens up. Truth is not universal—it is **personal**. Your truth is tied to your **facts** and your **desires**, but what often stands in the way of achieving what you want is the **frame**—the set of beliefs, rules, and stories you have built around your life.

Your **soul** is always seeking deeper expression. It is constantly pushing to **expand**, but the lies and stories you've built create **prisons** that keep you trapped. The key to liberation and elevation is understanding and **shattering your current frame.**

### The Frame Game: How Your Mind Creates Your Reality

Every human operates inside **a frame**—a lens through which they see the world. This frame dictates what they believe is **possible**, what they think is **right or wrong**, and what they think is **normal**.

Your **frame** is built from four key components:

1. **Laws** — *Deep-rooted subconscious rules that define your reality.*
2. **Rules** — *Self-imposed guidelines for what you will or won't do.*
3. **Beliefs** — *Perspectives you have adopted from past experiences.*
4. **Stories** — *The narratives you tell yourself to justify your current reality.*

These components create **your world**, but they also act as **prison bars**—keeping you from **expansion, success, and transformation**.

Examples of Frames:

- **Money Frame:** *"I can't afford it."* → **Reality:** You will never have money.
- **Relationship Frame:** *"Sex isn't important to me."* → **Reality:** Your intimacy is dying.
- **Health Frame:** *"I don't have time to work out."* → **Reality:** You're choosing weakness.
- **Business Frame:** *"People won't pay me that much."* → **Reality:** You undervalue yourself.

To change your life, you must break your frame.

### Breaking The Frame: The Process Of Transformation

#### Step 1: Expose the Frame as False

Your current frame feels **true**, but in reality, it is a **lie**. You must be willing to **challenge it.**

- A man says, **"I can't afford a $50,000 surgery."**
- His doctor tells him, **"If you don't get this surgery, you will die within 12 months."**
- Suddenly, the man finds the **$50,000 instantly**—because his **frame was shattered.**

People will defend their **frame** to the death, even when it is destroying them. Your job is to **penetrate that frame with truth**.

#### Step 2: Introduce a New Frame

Once the old frame is broken, a new frame must be built—one that aligns with the **truth and the reality they desire**.

- **OLD FRAME:** *"I'm not sexy. I don't feel confident in my body."*
  - **NEW FRAME:** *"I'm in the best shape of my life. I feel unstoppable."*
  - **Transformation:** *A woman competes in a fitness competition, builds confidence, and completely **redefines her sexuality and self-image.***

#### Step 3: Reinforce the New Frame with Truth

For a new frame to hold, it must be **rooted in truth**. Once someone experiences **results and fruit** in this new frame, the old world no longer makes sense.

- A man who once believed flying first class was "too expensive" now only flies private—because his frame of wealth and possibility **expanded**.
- A woman who believed she could "never afford help" now has two full-time nannies, **freeing her to be a present mother and wife**.

### How Your Frame Determines Your Reality

Imagine three people on the same flight to Dubai:

1. **Person A** is in **economy class**, cramped and miserable.
2. **Person B** is in **business class**, relaxed with a bed and champagne.
3. **Person C** is in **first class**, with a private suite, shower, and gourmet dining.

**Same plane, same destination, totally different realities.**

Your **life is the same way.**

- Some people live in a **scarcity frame**—always struggling.
- Others live in a **power frame**—always expanding.
- Your frame **dictates your experience, your results, and your reality.**

### How To Break Your Own Frame

Identify the areas where you are **stuck** in a false frame:

#### Real Facts — *What are the objective facts about your situation?*

- **"I'm making $100K a year."**
- **"My marriage lacks passion."**
- **"I'm 50 pounds overweight."**

#### Raw Feelings — *How do you actually feel about these facts?*

- **Frustrated? Powerless? Angry? Embarrassed?**

#### Relevant Focus — *What do you actually want?*

- **"I want to make $1M a year."**
- **"I want my wife to crave me sexually."**
- **"I want to be in the best shape of my life."**

#### Result-Driven Fruit — *What measurable result will prove your frame has changed?*

- **"I will generate $1M in revenue this year."**
- **"I will have sex 4 times a week with my wife."**
- **"I will drop 50 pounds and have visible abs."**

### The Sacred Truth: You Control Your Frame

Your **frame is your choice.** You are **not stuck**. You are simply choosing a world that limits you.

**What frame must you break today to step into your next level?**

Every **limitation is a lie**. The only thing standing between you and what you want is the **frame you are willing to destroy**.

The world belongs to those who **shatter frames and redefine reality.**

This is **The Warrior Method.** This is ***your path to power.*** And we are just getting started.

## Chapter 5: THE SOUL

If you can learn to **dominate in the darkness, you will prosper in the light.**

This principle is the foundation of transformation. Everyone wants to reach the **peak**, but most people refuse to enter the **pit**. They avoid the **darkness, pain, and confrontation** required for real change. The soul seeks expansion, but it is trapped by **false frames and limiting beliefs**. To break free, we must embrace the process of **Pit to Peak.**

### The Science Of Pit To Peak

Everything you desire exists **on the peak**—whether it's wealth, power, connection, or freedom. But most people are trapped in the **pit**, unaware of the prison they've built for themselves. This process is **scientific**—a repeatable framework that allows you to **break frames, create movement, and lead transformation.**

#### The Pit to Peak Framework

1. **Pit** — The place of pain, lies, and limitation. People either know they're in it or are unaware, sedated by distractions.
2. **Peak** — The place of power, expansion, and transformation.
3. **Path** — The bridge between the pit and peak, the structured process of change.
4. **Pitch** — The call to action, the invitation to step into the new reality.

### The Pit: Breaking The Illusion

People don't change when they're comfortable. They **must feel the pain** of where they are before they will move.

- Some people are aware of their **pit**—they're stuck, but they refuse to act.
- Others are **so sedated** that they don't even realize they are in the pit. These people need **awakening.**
- **Rock Bottom** is when the pain becomes undeniable. Some rise from it, while others remain trapped forever.

#### The Role of the Pit in Influence

- **80% of marketing, sales, and persuasion happens in the pit.**
- You must **shine light into the darkness**, making the pain visible.
- **Truth destroys illusion.** If someone refuses to see the truth, they will never leave the pit.
- **Victim mentality keeps people trapped.** "I deserve help. I deserve success. I deserve an easy life." No, you don't. You must **fight for it.**

Example:

- A man says, "I can't afford a $50,000 surgery."
- His doctor tells him, "If you don't, you will die in 12 months."
- Suddenly, he **finds the money**—because his **frame was shattered.**

If the pit isn't exposed, people will not move.

### The Peak: Creating The Vision

Once someone recognizes their **pit**, they must be shown the **peak**—a compelling vision of what's possible.

- The peak must be **clear, specific, and emotionally compelling.**
- People must **feel the pull of expansion.**
- **Example:**
  - **Pit:** A man feels unattractive and ignored by his wife.
  - **Peak:** Imagine your wife looking at you with pure desire, craving intimacy with you daily.

Most salespeople and leaders make the mistake of **only talking about the peak.** If there is no **contrast**, there is no movement.

### The Gap: Creating The Desire For The Path

When the contrast between **Pit and Peak** is clear, the natural question arises:

**"How do I get there?"**

This is where the **Path** is introduced. But here's the key:

**Once someone falls in love with the peak, they stop caring about the details of the path.**

- They don't care about **features and benefits.**
- They don't care about **step-by-step processes.**
- They only care that this is **the fastest way to reach their new reality.**

#### Example: Selling a Boat

1. **Pit:** A father feels disconnected from his kids.
2. **Peak:** He envisions creating lifelong memories with his children on a boat.
3. **Gap:** "How do I make this happen?"
4. **Path:** "This is the best boat for your vision."
5. **Pitch:** "Here's how we finalize it."

The boat isn't the product. The experience is.

This is **how seduction works**—not by selling features, but by creating **pull energy.**

### The Final Step: The Pitch

Once the **Pit to Peak** process has been established, the **final step is simple.**

- The **Path** has been revealed.
- The **Desire** has been built.
- The **Frame has been shattered.**

Now, all that's left is to **extend the invitation.**

**"Here's what to do next."**

#### The Rules of the Pitch:

1. **Never pitch without a gap.** If there's no contrast between pit and peak, the pitch falls flat.
2. **If there's no gift, there's no pitch.** The gift is the **path** that bridges the gap.
3. **Extend an invitation, not an obligation.** "This is for those who are ready. You don't have to do anything." This removes resistance.

Example:

- **Bad Pitch:** "Buy my program—it has 10 modules and 50 hours of content."
- **Great Pitch:** "If you want to become the man your wife craves, this is how we make it happen."

### The Formula For Irresistible Influence

**Pit** → **Peak** → **Path** → **Pitch**

1. **Expose the Pit** — Make the pain undeniable.
2. **Illuminate the Peak** — Create a compelling future.
3. **Expand the Gap** — The greater the contrast, the greater the movement.
4. **Reveal the Path** — Show the way forward.
5. **Deliver the Pitch** — Extend the invitation with certainty.

**If there is no gap, there is no gift. If there is no gift, there is no pitch.**

This is **Strategic Seduction.** This is **The Warrior Method.** And this is how you create **irresistible influence.**

## Chapter 13: THE 13 QUESTIONS — COACHING THROUGH CLARITY

In this chapter, we drill down into the beating heart of powerful coaching: *questions*. Not advice. Not content. Not frameworks. Questions.

Because in coaching, you're not the hero — the client is. And the fastest way to unlock their clarity, confidence, and action is to stop talking and start asking the right questions.

### ⚔️ The Foundation: Frame, Freedom, Focus, Fire

Before the questions, we anchor into a *four-part map* that becomes the coaching container:

1. **Frame** — Where are you right now?
2. **Freedom** — Where do you want to go?
3. **Focus** — What needs to happen in the next 30 days?
4. **Fire** — What must be done this week and today?

The 13 Questions fall inside this 4-step model and act like GPS markers that chart the course from prison to purpose.

### 🔥 Part 1: Frame — Entrance A (Questions 1–5)

This is where coaching begins — with facts, feelings, and truth about the current reality.

#### 1. Where are you right now?

- **Open-ended. No setup. No fluff.**
- **Ask and let them take you where they feel most alive or most stuck — body, business, balance, or being.**
- **This reveals *the terrain* of their life.**

#### 2. How do you feel about that?

- **This question moves from facts into feelings.**
- **Emotion gives power to change.**
- **They may start intellectual, but this question helps access the soul.**

#### 3. How did you get here?

- **This digs into story.**
- **You're looking for patterns, behaviors, beliefs, and turning points.**
- **This reveals their current operating system — the *frame* that created this life.**

#### 4. Is where you are working for you?

- **Simple yes or no.**
- **Most people *tolerate* a reality that doesn't serve them.**
- **This question shines a spotlight on whether their frame has become their prison.**

#### 5. Why is it working or not working?

- **Here we get reasons, rationalizations, and limiting beliefs.**
- **They'll begin to show you their "box" — the stories and rules that keep them stuck.**
- **Don't argue. Just extract.**

These five questions can loop back and forth as needed — they're not a linear checklist, they're a dance.

### 💫 Part 2: Freedom — Entrance B (Questions 6–10)

Now we shift from the pain of what is into the possibility of what *could be*. This section unlocks vision and desire.

#### 6. What do you want?

- **The most important — and most difficult — question.**
- **Most people haven't been asked this in years.**
- **Or they give you what they *don't* want instead. Bring them back to the yes.**

#### 7. Why do you want that?

- **Motivation behind the desire.**
- **Peel back the layers. Often, people say "money" when they mean "freedom," "love," or "significance."**
- **This creates *emotional attachment* to the vision.**

#### 8. How would it feel if you had it?

- **This brings the dream into the body.**
- **Now they're experiencing the *feeling* of freedom.**
- **Once they feel it, they'll fight for it.**

#### 9. What's in the way?

- **Every vision has a dragon.**
- **Ask them to identify the obstacles — internal and external.**
- **You're laying the groundwork for transformation and planning.**

#### 10. What's required to overcome that?

- **Now they step into ownership.**
- **You're not telling them what to do — you're guiding them to name it themselves.**
- ***When the solution comes from them, they own it.***

### 🔥 Part 3: Fire — Moving from Vision to Action (Questions 11–13)

This is where coaching becomes tactical. We map out real-world action that moves them from *vision* to *velocity.*

#### 11. What must be done first?

- **Narrow the focus.**
- **Remove overwhelm.**
- **This is the one domino that starts the chain.**

#### 12. How will we measure that?

- **You're turning feelings into fruit.**
- **No vagueness. No fluff. Just a measurable outcome.**
- **If it can't be measured, it can't be mastered.**

#### 13. When will it be done?

- **Anchor the action to a *time and date.***
- **This moves it from idea to commitment.**
- **This is where the fire begins.**

### 🎯 Bonus Questions (Used Liberally Throughout)

These are the *spice* of the conversation. You'll use them constantly to deepen and clarify:
